"Playfully Recycling for a Positive World: Earn Points, Make a Difference!"

The project aims to make recycling more enjoyable. The programme offers tasks to earn points that can be redeemed at specific locations. This could be, for example, throwing your rubbish in the right place. In summary, we want to get people to recycle in a fun and playful way.

What is our goals?

Recycling

 We aim to achieve sustainability through recycling, avoiding further damage to our Earth by sourcing materials and instead utilizing our existing belongings wisely

Clean environment

 By preserving a clean environment, we aim to protect our Earth and our own lives. Our bodies can be healthier, and we can lead a happier life.

Saving living beings

 Unfortunately, due to extensive pollution, many animals die, and numerous plants perish. However, by being mindful of what and how we dispose of waste, we could potentially save thousands of lives.

Laying the groundwork for our future

 Now is the time to make changes so that the future generations, and even we, can live in a much cleaner and more fulfilling environment.
